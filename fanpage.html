Motel Radio: Good Band

I'm not gonna lie, this group is weird, and I love what they do.


--Reasons to like such a band--


Motel Radio is an instrumentally, lyrically, and vocally talented group that provides a sound that is not only upbeat and enjoyable, but complex and thought provoking as well. The band never forgets that life is like a box of chocolates, and they never take themselves too seriously; they showcase this attribute in their hilarious music videos.
